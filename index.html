Biography

Dr. Gunwoong Park is an assistant professor in Statistics department at University of Seoul. He graduated in 2011 from Statistics department at Seoul National University. He obtained Ph.D. in Statistics department at UW - Madison under the supervision of Garvesh Raskutti and became a lecturer and post-doc fellow in Statistics department at University of Michigan under the joint supervision of Liza Levina and Ji Zhu.
